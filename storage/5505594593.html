Welcome!

I’m Michael Dimock, president of Pew Research Center, and I would like to extend a warm welcome to you as a new subscriber. Your interest in our work sustains us in our mission, which is to generate a foundation of facts that enriches the public dialogue and supports sound decision-making. 

What sets us apart
We are independent, nonprofit and nonpartisan. We do not advocate or take policy positions, and we endeavor to follow the facts where they lead. We make all of our work available to the public – for free. We are transparent about our methods (more on that in the next email in this series) and are constantly working to advance the field of survey research.

Our values
Independence, objectivity, accuracy, rigor, humility, transparency and innovation are core values for the Center and its employees. We strive to reflect these values in all that we do.

What we study
The Center explores topics ranging from politics and religion to science, technology, the news media, race and ethnicity and more. In a world awash with data, we offer a guide to people’s lives and voices by focusing on their concerns, behaviors, priorities, values and experiences. 

We want to hear from you
It is the enthusiasm of curious and engaged readers like you that continues to inspire us to study important issues that matter to you and to our fellow citizens around the globe. Is there a topic you'd like to see us explore or a question you want us to investigate? Just reply to this email – we'd love to hear from you. And you can always reach us at info@pewresearch.org. 

Best wishes,

Michael Dimock
President
Pew Research Center
