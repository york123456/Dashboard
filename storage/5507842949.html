﻿Reimagining the business

What if AI could remove the constraints shaping your business?

“Anything that was a constraint in my model right now might not be with agentic. If human capital, for example, is no longer a constraint, if the cost of intelligence or the cost of creativity is no longer a constraint, what then? That’s why I think this reimagined moment is more important now than it was in the first book,” explains Kate Smaje, McKinsey senior partner and coauthor of the second edition of Rewired (Rewired: How Leading Companies Win with Technology and AI, Wiley, April 2026).

In this video, Smaje and her coauthors, Eric Lamarre, McKinsey special adviser, and Robert Levin, McKinsey senior partner, explore how agentic AI could reshape core business systems across industries. From continuous personalized marketing to AI-enabled transformation in healthcare, the authors discuss what becomes possible when leaders challenge long-held assumptions about existing processes—and reimagine where and how value is created.
