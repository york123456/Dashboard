Dear Readers,


As the pace of change in markets accelerates, one constant remains: the strength of a professional community grounded in discipline, education, and shared purpose. This month’s issue of Technically Speaking reflects that spirit across every article, event recap, and leadership update featured in these pages.


We begin with a thoughtful message from the Board by Eric Caisse, CMT, CFA, CFP, ABFPSM, whose reflections remind us that leadership often means navigating both triumphs and setbacks with perspective and resilience. That same sense of stewardship carries throughout this issue as members are encouraged to participate in the open election process ahead of CMT Association’s Annual General Meeting. The future of our profession depends on engaged members willing to shape it.


Community is the defining theme this month. From the global conversations sparked at the Spring Summit in Boston to the launch of the Los Angeles Community chapter, CMT Association continues to demonstrate the value of bringing practitioners together to exchange ideas, build relationships, and advance technical analysis worldwide. These gatherings are more than networking opportunities — they are proof that our field thrives through collaboration and mentorship.


This issue also highlights the growing influence of technical analysis within academia through coverage of SMIFC South 2026.
