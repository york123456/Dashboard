WATCH: Artemis II Lunar Science Discussion at NASA Goddard – June 8

 

NASA Goddard Space Flight Center invites you to attend a virtual panel discussion featuring members of the Artemis II Lunar Science Team.

 

Join us as they share first-hand insights and experiences from this historic mission.

NASA Goddard played a pivotal role in the success of Artemis II, from maintaining critical crew communications with Earth and monitoring space weather, to leading core lunar science operations. Our team’s contributions included astronaut training, designing the lunar observation campaign, and providing real-time operational support from Mission Control.
