🇺🇸 WEEK IN REVIEW

Violent Crime Plummeted at the Fastest Rate in Nearly 90 Years according to a new FBI report, with officials confirming that the Trump administration's tough-on-crime policies are working. Communities across America are safer, and the results speak for themselves — this is what happens when law enforcement is fully backed by the President of the United States.


The Administration's Anti-Fraud Momentum is Accelerating as President Trump's task force continues its work targeting an estimated $250 billion in government fraud — and officials say it is just getting started. Simultaneously, HHS deployed artificial intelligence to detect Medicaid fraud and waste, bringing cutting-edge technology to bear in the fight to protect taxpayer dollars.


President Trump is Rebuilding America's Workforce and Economy on multiple fronts this week. The administration launched Workforce Pell Grants to fast-track Americans into high-demand skilled trades, reversed Biden-era refrigerant regulations to lower grocery costs, and committed $2 billion to quantum-computing firms to ensure America leads the next technological revolution. The DOJ also exposed a two-decade-long election fraud scheme in California, validating the administration's push for election integrity.


America's Faith is on Full Display as thousands packed the National Mall for an America 250 prayer rally where President Trump read from the Bible — a powerful testament to the nation's spiritual revival. As we mark Memorial Day, we honor the fallen heroes whose sacrifice made this resurgence possible — and we recommit to building the America they died defending.


The UAE Made History This Week by Exiting OPEC in a landmark diplomatic victory for President Trump, delivering a major shift in global energy markets that is expected to increase oil supply and put downward pressure on energy prices for American families. The move reflects the growing influence of President Trump's America First energy agenda on the world stage.
