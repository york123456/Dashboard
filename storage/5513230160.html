Right-wing populism in the decade since Brexit

 

British voters’ decision in 2016 to leave the European Union came as a shock to many in the United Kingdom and beyond. The “leave” campaign tapped into anti-elite sentiment, economic anxieties and other social discontents that have since driven up support for right-wing populist parties in Europe and elsewhere.

  • In several European countries, EU gets more positive ratings today than during Brexit vote

 
 

Trading volume on prediction markets has soared in recent months

 

Combined monthly trading volume on Kalshi and Polymarket rose from less than $5 billion in September 2025 to about $24 billion in April 2026. Sports, politics and cryptocurrency are the topics that see the highest trading volume on both leading prediction markets.

  • Americans increasingly see legal sports betting as a bad thing for society and sports

 
 

Quiz: How much do you know about NATO?

 

NATO has been in the news a lot this year – but how much do Americans know about the alliance? Take our short quiz to test your NATO knowledge, then compare your results to those of the U.S. public as a whole.

  • Republicans have become less likely to say NATO membership benefits the U.S.

 
 

What political issues do Americans hear about in church?

 

Two-thirds of U.S. adults who regularly attend religious services say they have heard their clergy speak about at least one political or social issue in the past few months. Abortion, Israel and homosexuality came up most often out of the seven topics we asked about.

  • How Americans feel about religion’s influence in government and public life

 
 

Americans are increasingly pessimistic about avoiding the worst effects of climate change

 

A growing share of Americans (62%) say the U.S. and other countries will not do enough to avoid the worst effects of climate change. While majorities say extreme weather events have become more frequent (68%) and more severe (62%), partisans differ in their perceptions, even within the same regions of the country.
